1. SCOPE OF WORK

The Developer agrees to build, host, and maintain a custom e-commerce platform tailored to the Client's brand.

Deliverable: A high-performance Progressive Web Application (PWA) optimized for all devices, including an administrative dashboard for order management and an integrated customer notification system.

Scope Freeze: This agreement covers the deployment of the standard architecture customized with the Client's Logo, Brand Colors, Product Inventory, and specific Business Logic Configurations.

Out of Scope: Additional feature requests or structural changes outside this scope will be evaluated and billed separately at an agreed project rate.

2. FINANCIAL TERMS (THE ZERO-SETUP SaaS MODEL)

In lieu of traditional upfront development and monthly maintenance fees, the Developer and Client agree to a revenue-share model:

  • Setup Fee: GHS 0.00 (Zero upfront cost to the Client).
  • Monthly Maintenance Fee: .
  • Platform Commission: The Developer shall receive a commission on the gross total of every successful transaction processed through the application.
  • Payment Automation: This commission will be automatically routed to the Developer's account at the exact moment of purchase via the Paystack "Split Payment" system. The Client will automatically receive the remaining into their designated settlement account.
  • Payment Processing Fees: The standard gateway processing fee (e.g., Paystack's 1.95%) will be absorbed by the end-customer at checkout as a "Processing Fee." This ensures the Client's revenue split and product margins remain fully protected.

  • Free Tiers: We launch on Free Tiers (Cloudflare, Supabase, Cloudinary) supporting ~1,000 orders/month.
  • Scaling: If traffic exceeds free limits, the Client must upgrade server tiers directly via the cloud provider (typically ~$25 USD/month).

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & LICENSING

A. Ownership of Deliverables: The Client is granted exclusive ownership of the specific Branded Application instance created for their business, all User Data (customers, orders) generated by the application, and their visual branding assets.

B. Retention of Background IP: The Developer retains full ownership of the "Architecture" (the underlying source code, database structure, and admin dashboard logic). The Client is granted a Non-Exclusive, Non-Transferable License to use this code for the operation of their business for the duration of this agreement.

C. White Label Protection: The Client MAY NOT resell, license, rent, or distribute the underlying source code to third parties.

5. INCLUDED MAINTENANCE SERVICES

As long as the platform commission is active, the Developer agrees to provide the following ongoing services:

  • Server Uptime Monitoring: Ensuring the website and API are online and accessible.
  • Security Patches: Applying critical security updates to the backend and database.
  • Database Management: Regular backups of customer and order data.
  • Third-Party API Maintenance: Ensuring connections to Paystack, Email providers, and media servers remain functional.

Note: This does not cover the development of new features, redesigns, or content entry (adding products).
